# NearBell product notes

Short product notes for arrival alert decisions.

### Daily product note 01

NearBell should keep route alerts understandable at a glance. This note records a small product decision for the extension: prefer clear, local, permission-aware feedback over noisy automation. A navigation helper is only useful when it tells the user what changed, why it matters, and whether an alert can still fire safely.
