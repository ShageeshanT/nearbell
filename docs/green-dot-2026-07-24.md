## Theme

Schedule slot helpers. After the traffic and condition helpers
from 2026-07-21, NearBell needs a clean scheduling layer: when can
an alert fire, how big is the window, does it conflict with other
slots, and how far through the window are we right now.

## Helpers added

- `scheduleSlotForAlert` — absolute ISO timestamp for a future alert slot
- `scheduleWindowLength` — minute span of a scheduling window
- `scheduleWindowOverlaps` — boolean overlap check for two windows
- `scheduleSlotLabel` — short copy like "in 15m" / "in 1h15m"
- `scheduleSlotBucket` — early-morning / morning / afternoon / evening / night
- `scheduleNextSlotFromNow` — first slot strictly in the future
- `scheduleSlotConflicts` — tolerance-based conflict check
- `scheduleSlotMinutesUntil` — floor of minutes between now and target
- `scheduleWindowIsFull` — has the window hit its slot cap?
- `scheduleSlotIsPast` — is the target offset in the past?
- `scheduleSlotProgressPercent` — 0-100 progress through the window
